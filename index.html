About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Mathematics at Simon Fraser University and recently completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Ottawa. I place myself at the intersection of Combinatorics and Cryptography, with a special focus on developing new combinatorial objects with nice applications in cryptography.







Experience:

  • Postdoctoral fellow - Mathematics Department - Simon Fraser University (nov 2019 - now)
  • Ph.D. degree in Computer Science - University of Ottawa (Sept 2015 - Sept 2019)
  • Master's degree in Computer Science - Federal University of Santa Catarina (Mar 2013 - Jun 2015)
  • Bachelor degree in Computer Science - Federal University of Santa Catarina (Mar 2009 - Dec 2012)
